Bahn Mi

Ingredients

  • one french baguette
  • sliced into sticks cucumber
  • pickle carrots
  • chili mayo
    • -
  • Meat: chicken, pork or beef

Step by Step:

  1. Cut the vegetables to sticks
  2. marinated the meat for about 20m
  3. toast the baguette
  4. Cut the baguette and layered it with sauce
  5. add the vegetables in and let it wait
  6. Pull out the meat and cut it into bit size pieces
  7. Grill the meat to it becomes crispy
  8. assemble the rest of the sandwich with the meat on the side
  9. East and enjoy
