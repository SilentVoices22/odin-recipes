Recipe for Congee

is incredibly simple at its core—just rice and lots of water—but it is slow-cooked until the grains break down into a silky, creamy porridge.
Here is a basic stovetop recipe for a savory, flavorful congee base, along with popular topping ideas.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 8 to 10 cups of water
  • optional to replace it with broth for more flavor
  • 1 large slice of ginger
  • pinch of salt or a splash of soy sauce

Step by step guide:

  1. Rinse the rice
  2. Put it on a cooker or rice cooker
  3. Fill it with water or broth
  4. add the slice of ginger in the broth
  5. turn on the heat to medium
  6. let it cooked about 20 minutes
  7. plate your plate, and is ready to eat
