Ramen

ingredients

  • noodles one or two serving
  • Stock any kind of stock is good
  • Meat is optional
  • frozen veggies one cup
  • salt 1 tsp
  • one egg

Step by Step guide:

Simple Ramen, that can be done in under 5 minuteThat's a great choice!
Ramen can be surprisingly simple to make at home. Here is an easy, quick chicken ramen recipe that focuses on building a flavorful
broth without hours of simmering.

  1. Fill the pot with decent amount of water or broth
  2. pre add the seasoning packets
  3. let the water boil in medium hot temp
  4. add the noodles
  5. add a egg
  6. add the optional to make it more boujee
  7. let it shimmer for 10 before eating
  8. is ready to eat, whether straight from the pot or a bowl
